About Our Society

Learn more about our rich heritage and community values

Established in 1985

Our society was founded by visionary community leaders who wanted to preserve our cultural heritage and create a platform for Punjabi Khatri families to come together, support each other, and celebrate our traditions.

Founded By

Shri Ram Lal Kapoor Ji, along with other respected elders of the community, laid the foundation of this society with a vision to unite all Punjabi Khatri families under one umbrella and promote cultural harmony.

Our Mission

To unite Punjabi Khatri families, preserve our cultural heritage, and support community members through various social and cultural initiatives. We aim to create lasting bonds across generations.

What We Do

We organize cultural events, festivals, sports activities, and social gatherings throughout the year. We also provide a platform for networking and support among community members.

Our Community

Our society comprises five major family domains - Kapoor, Khatri, Tandon, Bhalla, and Bajaj. Each family contributes to the rich tapestry of our community with their unique traditions.
